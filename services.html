FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a house? +
Typically 4–12 months depending on the size and complexity of the project. A simple 3-bedroom bungalow may take 5–6 months, while a large villa can take up to a year. We provide a detailed timeline before starting.
Do you offer payment plans? +
Yes. We offer flexible milestone-based payment plans — typically 30% deposit, then payments tied to construction milestones (foundation, roofing, finishing, handover). We'll discuss what works for your situation.
Can you work with my own architect's plans? +
Absolutely. We're happy to build from your existing approved plans, or collaborate with your architect. We also offer our own in-house design team if you don't have plans yet.
What warranties do you offer? +
We provide a 1-year structural warranty on all completed projects. Any defects arising from our workmanship within this period will be rectified at no cost to you.
Do you work outside Dar Es Salaam? +
Yes! We handle projects across Tanzania including Mwanza, Dodoma, Arusha, and other regions. Transport and accommodation costs for our team are factored into the project quote.
