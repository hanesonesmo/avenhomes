Common Questions

Quick Answers

How quickly will you respond to my message?

We typically respond to all messages within 24 hours on business days. For urgent matters, call or WhatsApp us directly for an instant response.

Do you provide free quotations?

Yes! Initial consultations and quotations are completely free. We will assess your project and provide a detailed, transparent estimate with no obligation.

Can you visit my plot before quoting?

Absolutely. For construction projects, we prefer to conduct a site visit to give you the most accurate and tailored quotation possible.

Do you work outside Dar Es Salaam?

Yes, we serve all regions of Tanzania including Arusha, Mwanza, Dodoma, Mbeya, and beyond. Travel costs are included in project quotes.
